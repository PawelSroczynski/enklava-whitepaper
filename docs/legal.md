# Legal Framework

## Token Classification

The EKLV token is a **cryptographically secured utility token** designed for the Enklava Ecosystem.

### Regulatory Status

| Jurisdiction | Classification |
|--------------|---------------|
| **EU (MiCA)** | Utility Token (not a security) |
| **Poland (KNF)** | Not a financial instrument |

The token:
- Does NOT constitute a share or equity
- Does NOT represent ownership in any company
- DOES provide access to digital and physical services
