## Kinterra Performance Vesting

**Allocation:** 10% / 1,000,000 EKLV

Kinterra's tokens vest based on achieving specific business milestones, not time:

| Milestone | EKLV Released | Trigger |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| **Seed Close** | 250,000 (25%) | Kinterra closes €300K+ fundraising round |
| **First Sales** | 250,000 (25%) | First 10 Climate Fortress units sold globally |
| **Revenue Target** | 250,000 (25%) | €1,000,000 cumulative hardware revenue |
| **Network Scale** | 250,000 (25%) | 3+ regional franchise partners onboarded |

### Failure Clause

If the Kinterra partnership terminates before all milestones are achieved:
- **Unvested tokens return to Foundation Reserve**
- Foundation may reallocate to new partners or burn
- No clawback of already-vested tokens

## Circulating Supply Schedule

Not all tokens are available immediately. This schedule shows when tokens enter circulation:

| Timeframe | Event | New Tokens | Total Circulating | % of Supply |
|-----------|-------|------------|-------------------|-------------|
| **Day 1** | Liquidity Pool locked | 1,000,000 | 1,000,000 | 10% |
| **Month 6** | Seed cliff ends | +83,333/mo | 1,500,000 | 15% |
| **Month 12** | Founder cliff ends | +31,250/mo | 2,000,000 | 20% |
| **Month 12** | Tech Alpha milestone | +165,000 | 2,165,000 | 21.6% |
| **Year 2** | Various vests continue | ~+1,200,000 | 3,400,000 | 34% |
| **Year 3** | Liquidity incentives complete | ~+1,000,000 | 5,500,000 | 55% |
| **Year 4** | Founder fully vested | +remaining | 7,000,000 | 70% |
| **Year 5** | Foundation unlocks | +1,500,000 | 10,000,000 | 100% |

### Key Insight

At launch, only **10% of supply** is circulating (the liquidity pool). This creates:
- Lower initial market cap for same FDV
- Reduced sell pressure during early growth
- Gradual distribution as ecosystem matures

## Control Architecture

| Block | Allocation | Alignment |
|-------|------------|-----------|
| Founder | 15% | Core Team |
| Foundation Reserve | 15% | Core (founder-managed, legally separated) |
| Tech Team | 5% | Core Team |
| Liquidity Incentives | 10% | Neutral (distributed to LPs) |
| **Friendly Block** | **45%** | Aligned with long-term protocol success |

### Decentralization Path

The 45% "Friendly Block" ensures protocol stability during early growth while remaining below majority control.

**Over time:**
- Community (25%) distributes via Toloka contributions
- Liquidity Incentives (10%) distribute to liquidity providers
- Partners vest based on performance (may not fully vest)

**Result:** Governance naturally decentralizes as the ecosystem matures.
