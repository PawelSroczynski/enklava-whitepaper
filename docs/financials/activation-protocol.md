# Activation Protocol

The activation protocol bootstraps Enklava through a **self-liquidating content funnel** that builds community and capital simultaneously.

---

## The Writing in Public Model

### Philosophy

> "The yurt is not the product. **Enklava is the story. The yurt is Chapter 1.**"

Content is released progressively, building audience while generating revenue. Paid subscribers get real-time access; free content follows 12 months later.

## Activation Phases

### Phase 1: Calibration (Weeks 1-4)

**Objective:** Find profitable ad configuration

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Budget | 1,500 PLN (~€350) |
| CPL Target | Under 20 PLN |
| Leads | 75-100 |
| OTO Conversion | 15%+ |

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] Squeeze page live
- [ ] Lead magnet (3 chapters PDF)
- [ ] OTO page + ROI Calculator
- [ ] Email sequence (cold leads)
- [ ] First video ad
- [ ] Meta Ads campaign

**Success Criteria:** RPL exceeds CPL (profitable configuration found)

---

### Phase 2: Stabilization (Months 2-3)

**Objective:** Scale to sustainable lead flow

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Budget | 2,000-3,000 PLN/mo (~€470-700) |
| Leads | 200+/month |
| Community | First 50 subscribers |
| Revenue | Break-even or positive |

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] Upsell/Downsell pages
- [ ] Avatar-segmented email sequences
- [ ] Builder's Circle community launch
- [ ] First 3 blog chapters (SEO)
- [ ] Retargeting campaigns

**Success Criteria:** Consistent 200+ leads/month with positive ROI

---

### Phase 3: Evergreen (Month 4+)

**Objective:** Self-sustaining growth machine

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Budget | Scales with profitability |
| Leads | 500+/month |
| Community | 100+ subscribers |
| High-Ticket | First KIT/Turnkey sales |

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] Organic traffic growing (SEO)
- [ ] Blended CPL decreasing
- [ ] High-ticket sales pipeline active
- [ ] Avatar D (Enklava Builder) conversions

**Success Criteria:** Funnel generates capital for infrastructure development

## Transition to Hardware

When the funnel reaches Phase 3 stability, capital flows shift:

```
PHASE 1-2: Funnel Revenue → Funnel Growth
PHASE 3+:  Funnel Revenue → Infrastructure Development
                          → First Climate Fortress build
                          → Hospitality revenue begins
                          → Token buybacks activate
```

The funnel doesn't stop — it becomes the **lead generation engine** for high-ticket hardware sales orchestrated through Kinterra.
