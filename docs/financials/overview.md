# Financial Overview

## Key Financial Principles

### 1. Self-Liquidating Funnel

The content funnel is designed to be **cash-flow positive from Day 1**. Ad spend is recovered through immediate product sales, not future promises.

**Target:** Revenue Per Lead > Cost Per Lead

### 2. Gross Revenue Buybacks

Token buybacks are calculated on **Gross Revenue**, not Net Profit. This ensures:
- Transparency (no accounting games)
- Predictability (investors can model)
- Alignment (revenue = buybacks, always)

### 3. Operational Sovereignty

The Zadzim Living Lab is designed to reach **break-even on hospitality alone**. External capital (Kinterra, investors) accelerates growth but is not required for survival.

### 4. Terminal Backstop

Even in worst-case dissolution, the Foundation mandate ensures physical assets are liquidated to execute a **Terminal Buyback** for all token holders.
