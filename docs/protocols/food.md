## Soil Building

The real investment is underground.

### The Living Soil Protocol

| Practice | Purpose | Frequency |
|----------|---------|-----------|
| **No-dig gardening** | Preserve soil structure | Permanent |
| **Composting** | Feed soil biology | Continuous |
| **Cover cropping** | Nitrogen fixation, erosion | Off-season |
| **Mulching** | Moisture, temperature, biology | Permanent |
| **Biochar** | Carbon sequestration, retention | Once |

**Result:** Soil improves yearly. Yields increase. Inputs decrease.
